Christopher Stein

always curious, ever changing, grounded in reality

I teach front-end web development and design at BMCC in the department of Media Arts & Technology. A large part of my time goes into giving our students a solid, up-to-date education in modern web practices. I'm also the Director of User Experience here on the CUNY Academic Commons. You may also see me at some of the NYC Meetups related to the web.
